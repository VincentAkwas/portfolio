About Me

manu_vincent.profile

I'm a Medical Laboratory Scientist and self-taught developer with a passion for using technology to transform how lab science is taught, practiced, and understood. Based in Ghana, I create tools that bridge the gap between clinical laboratory knowledge and the people who need it most.

My work spans AI-powered lab result interpreters, psychometric assessments, daily case-based learning platforms, and a blog that covers everything from diagnostic techniques to patient-facing guides — all free and open to everyone.

My mission is simple: democratise medical laboratory knowledge across Africa and beyond, one tool at a time.

The most comprehensive medical lab result interpreter available free online.
